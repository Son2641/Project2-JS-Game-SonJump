Instructions:

  • Use your left and right arrow keys or 'a' and 'd' in your keyboard to move Son Goku.
  • Son Goku's goal is to jump on the moving platforms and reach the score of 5000.
  • Score is added based on the number of platforms eliminated
  • One platform eliminated is equivalent to 100 points.
  • The speed of the game increases as your score goes up.
